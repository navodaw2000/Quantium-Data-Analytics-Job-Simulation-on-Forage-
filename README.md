# Quantium Data Analytics Simulation – Forage

**Platform:** Forage  
**Program:** Quantium – Data Analytics & Commercial Insights  
**Role:** Data Analytics Intern Simulation  

---

## Overview
This simulation provided practical experience in **data analytics, customer insights, and commercial strategy** within a data science team setting. The tasks emphasized the **end-to-end process** of data preparation, analysis, and evidence-based recommendations to drive business outcomes.  

---

## Objectives
- Apply analytical techniques to real-world commercial datasets  
- Extract actionable insights from customer transaction data  
- Conduct uplift testing to assess trial store layouts  
- Deliver concise, impactful reports for decision-making  

---

## Skills Developed
- Data preparation & cleaning  
- Customer segmentation & analytics  
- Statistical analysis & uplift testing  
- Data visualization & reporting  
- Commercial insight generation  
- Evidence-based decision making  

---

## Outcome & Impact
- Gained practical, hands-on experience in a data analytics workflow  
- Developed the ability to **translate data into actionable business insights**  
- Enhanced skills in **reporting, commercial recommendations, and strategic thinking**
